# About Anti-Pest Solutions

Anti-Pest Solutions is an independent, owner-managed pest control business serving the
Hawkesbury region and surrounding communities.

Our staff are all highly trained and have many years' experience eradicating pests from
both residential and commercial properties.

## Owner/Operator

The business is owned and operated by Josh Herron, who brings extensive qualifications
and hands-on experience in pest management to every job.

## Our Approach

We develop a customised pest management strategy for every property, designed to
eliminate infestations comprehensively. We understand that pest problems are stressful,
so we aim to provide swift, professional service that fits around your schedule.

## Environmental Commitment

We prioritise eco-conscious pest control, using only APVMA-approved, environmentally
safe products. Our staff have the ecological knowledge to maintain the environmental
balance of your property while treatments do their job.
