# Residential Pest Control

Anti-Pest Solutions offers a comprehensive and discreet service for household pest
control, with rapid response times and solutions built around you.

## Pests We Treat

- Rodents
- Spiders
- Cockroaches
- Fleas
- Ants
- Bed bugs
- Birds and bird lice
- Stored product pests (weevils)
- Bees and wasps

## Our Approach

We pest-proof homes through an integrated pest management program that sets out clear
responsibilities for both homeowners and technicians - from the initial service through
to routine preventative visits - using environmentally sustainable practices throughout.

## What You Get

After an assessment of your property, we provide:

- Treatment installation
- A detailed report identifying any cleaning or repairs needed
- Prevention measures against future infestations

## Why It Matters

Left untreated, household pests bring unhygienic droppings and odours, food poisoning
bacteria, property and electrical damage, and the risk of bites or stings. Our team
stays current with training and legislation to protect your home, family and pets.
