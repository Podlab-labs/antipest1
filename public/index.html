European Wasp

on April 9, 2026

Whilst doing a routine General Pest Service on a home in Bowen Mountain on Monday, I discovered a European Wasp nest forming inside a cavity of the home.

Luckily for the home owner, I had arrived on the day, as this could have escalated to something much worse over the coming weeks.

This wasp species is dangerous and should be approached with care. Professional pest services should be called.

If you have any issues with pests in and around your home or business, please don’t hesitate to call Josh at Anti-Pest Solutions.
